OUR PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy is an important issue for all of us.  www.SpratlingSilver.com does not collect personally identifiable information in any circumstances other than when one orders a book: PayPal provides us only with shipping information, but never includes private credit card data. None of this information is retained or provided to third parties  (Books may be purchased by check or money order as well as by credit card through PayPal.)  

We do use "cookies."  Again, none provide personally identifiable information, but they do indicate to us such information as to which portions of this website are used most frequently, time of day the site is most used, search engines used and the words used to find our site.  We review this information so that we can constantly improve the design and content of our site to better meet your needs.
